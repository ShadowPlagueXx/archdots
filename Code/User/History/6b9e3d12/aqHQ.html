What this site's about

This is a hub for students to find interesting places in the university. New students, welcome! And returning students, you may find some places or information you didn't know before, so feel free to browse and take a look!

Why was this site made?

As a Malaysian, I am a big fan of good food. After coming to Sheffield, I made it a mission to seek out the best food places on campus. All over the city, there are plenty of options for students to find delicious meals, ensuring that no student will lack for food. Click on the bar above to start exploring!

Quiz?

As you may have noticed, there's a quiz page! Don't worry, they should be easy if you just take note of the names of the places you're reading about. Head sover there once you're done going through the rest of the info, good luck!
