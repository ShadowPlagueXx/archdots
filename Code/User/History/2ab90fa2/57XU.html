Where to eat - a quick sidenote

The Sheffield campus is spread out over a large area - be sure to check the locations of the venues before deciding to head there for lunch. It wouldn't be ideal to get somewhere and realise you only have a few minutes to finish your lunch before rushing back for your next lecture!

Diamond Kitchen

Located in the Diamond Building, the Diamond Kitchen serves a wide selection of burritos, wraps alongside meal deals and a cafe serving coffee & hot chocolate.

Directions to the Diamond Kitchen

Grill & Go

Located in the Students' Union, Grill & Go serves a wide selection of burritos, wraps for an affordable price.

Directions to Grill & Go

Wave Kitchen

Located in the Wave Building, the Wave Kitchen serves a varied selection of hot and cold food & drinks.

Directions to the Wave Kitchen

Café 1828

Located right smack in the middle of the medical school, this cafe is perfect for med students to stop by for breakfast or lunch for an affordable price.

Directions to Café 1828

Edge Bar

Located in the Edge building, between the Ranmoor and Endcliffe residences, the Edge Bar is a perfect social hub for students to unwind, serving modern pub food & drinks.

Directions to the Edge Bar

IC Café

Located in the Information Commons library building, this cafe is great for a quick bite before getting back to studying.

Directions to the IC Café

Inox

Located in the Students' Union, Inox provides all day dining, and is suitable for meetings and social working.

Directions to Inox

Heartspace Café

Located in the Engineering Heartspace, and featuring hot food, cookies, cakes and snacks, it is a great place for engineering students to frequent.

Directions to the IC Café
