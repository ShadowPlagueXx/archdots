Looking for picnic spots?

I've gotcha! There are many beautiful parks around campus that would be great for an afternoon out, bring some sandwiches and have a great time!

Endcliffe Park

For students staying in Endcliffe or Ranmoor, this nearby park is a popular spot in the summer to sit back and relax with friends. Fun fact, this park was opened in 1887 to commemorate the jubilee of Queen Victoria!

Botanical Gardens

Although it is a bit of a walk from the city, visiting the Botanical Gardens is a must, especially when its warm. It's beauty is sure to help take your mind off the stress of your studies, albeit for a moment.

Weston Park

Located in close proximity to the UoS campus, Weston Park makes for its size in its beauty. In the summer, it has a great atmosphere and an ice cream van is the cherry on the cake.

Crookes Valley Park

Also close to the University is Crookes Valley Park. It is in fact located right next to Weston Park! It has picsteresque views with cherry blossom trees along with the backdrop of its pond.
